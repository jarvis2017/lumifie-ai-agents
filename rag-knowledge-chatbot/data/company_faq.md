# Northwind Tools — Company FAQ

Welcome to Northwind Tools. This FAQ answers the questions employees and
customers ask us most often. If you can't find what you need here, email
support@northwindtools.example or ask in the #help channel.

## About the company

Northwind Tools is a B2B supplier of precision hand tools and workshop
equipment, founded in 2014 and headquartered in Columbus, Ohio. We ship to
customers across North America and the EU.

## Business hours and support

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern
Time. We do not offer phone support on weekends, but you can submit a ticket at
any time and we respond to all tickets within one business day.

## Orders and shipping

Standard orders ship within two business days. We offer free standard shipping
on orders over $250. Expedited (next-business-day) shipping is available at
checkout for an additional fee. Tracking numbers are emailed automatically once
an order leaves our warehouse.

## Returns and refunds

Unused items in their original packaging may be returned within 30 days of
delivery for a full refund. Items returned after 30 days but within 60 days are
eligible for store credit only. To start a return, open a ticket with your order
number and we will email you a prepaid shipping label. Refunds are issued to the
original payment method within five business days of us receiving the return.

## Warranty

All Northwind power tools carry a two-year limited warranty against manufacturing
defects. Hand tools carry a lifetime warranty. The warranty does not cover normal
wear, misuse, or damage from unauthorized repairs.

## Bulk and wholesale pricing

Customers ordering more than 50 units of a single item qualify for wholesale
pricing. Contact sales@northwindtools.example for a quote. Wholesale orders are
invoiced on net-30 terms once an account is approved.

## Account and billing

You can update payment methods and view past invoices from the Billing page in
your account dashboard. Invoices are emailed on the first of each month for
accounts on subscription plans.
