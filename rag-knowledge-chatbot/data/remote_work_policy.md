# Northwind Tools — Remote Work & Time-Off Policy

_Effective January 1, 2026. Applies to all full-time employees._

## Purpose

This policy describes how Northwind Tools supports remote and hybrid work and how
employees request and accrue paid time off. It exists so expectations are clear
and consistent across teams.

## Eligibility for remote work

All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day onboarding period are
eligible to work remotely. New hires work on-site during onboarding so they can
meet their team and learn our systems.

## Hybrid schedule

Northwind operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office
at least two days per week, typically Tuesday and Thursday, to support
collaboration. Teams may agree on different anchor days with their manager.

## Core hours

Regardless of location, all employees should be reachable during core hours of
10:00 AM to 3:00 PM Eastern Time. Outside core hours, employees may flex their
schedule to fit personal needs as long as their work is completed.

## Equipment and expenses

Northwind provides a laptop and an annual $500 home-office stipend for remote
employees to spend on a desk, chair, or monitor. Submit receipts through the
expense portal for reimbursement within 30 days of purchase.

## Paid time off (vacation)

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per year, accrued monthly at
a rate of 1.67 days per month. Up to 5 unused vacation days may be carried over
into the next calendar year; any balance above 5 days is forfeited on December 31.

To request vacation, submit the request through the HR portal at least two weeks
in advance. Your manager approves or declines within three business days. Vacation
during the December holiday freeze requires director approval.

## Sick leave

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, separate from vacation. Sick days do
not carry over. For absences longer than three consecutive days, a doctor's note
is required.

## Public holidays

Northwind observes 11 public holidays per year. The holiday calendar is published
each January on the HR portal.
